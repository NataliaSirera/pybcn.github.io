Python & Robotics on the 16th of January 2014!

The next upcoming Python Barcelona event will take place on the 16th of January 2014, it will take place at Parc de Recerca Biomèdica de Barcelona (PRBB) c/ Dr. Aiguader, 88, Barcelona. Room : "Ramón y Cajal"

Sign in on the Meetup Page
